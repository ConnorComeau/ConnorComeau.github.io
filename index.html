How to install Upsetti Spaghetti Modpack

  1. Download and install ATLauncher
  2. Download the modpack from discord or here. Do not unzip the file.

Open ATLauncher

  1. Click the Accounts tab on the right-hand side
  2. Login to your minecraft/microsoft account

Install the modpack

  1. Click the Instances tab on the right-hand side
  2. Click the Import button on the top left

  1. Find where you downloaded the modpack zip file.
  2. Click on the zip file then hit Open

Dedicate more RAM

  1. On the Instances tab, find the modpack we just added and click Settings

  1. Click on the Java/Minecraft tab at the top.
  2. Under Memory/Ram make sure to dedicate yourself 6-8 gigs (4000-8000)
  3. Click Save

You are all set. Click play on the modpack and have fun!
